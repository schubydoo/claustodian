Design rationale

The name fuses claustr- (the enclosure, the keeper) with custodian (guardian of a record). The mark is a “C” drawn as a fanned deck — a solid front C with a lighter C held behind it (a prior version kept in the stack) — and a forward chevron in its mouth (progression to the next version). Together: the C is the custodian/keeper, the layered deck is the versioned record, and the chevron is movement through versions. The open right side keeps the silhouette a clear C; at 16px the favicon drops the back layer to a single crisp C + chevron.

Colour. A single indigo (#3b5bdb, with a #b7c4fa ghost for depth, lightening to #748ffc on dark) carried straight from the live dataset site, so the logo and the published data feel like one system. Category hues (flag, env, cmd, config) stay reserved for the data UI, never the logo. Type. Inter Bold, lowercase, tight tracking — matching the repo/npm name claustodian.
